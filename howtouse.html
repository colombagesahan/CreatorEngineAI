How to Create Videos

Step 1: Choose Your Strategy

Quick Mode: Uses AI voices. Best for traffic generation.
Money Mode: Uses YOUR voice. Best for YouTube Partner Program (AdSense) as it's considered "original content".

Step 2: Generate Script

Select a country and enter a topic (or use "Auto-Find Trend"). Click "ENTER STUDIO" to let AI write the script for you.

Step 3: The Studio Editor

  • Visuals: You MUST upload an image or video for every scene. Click the "Upload Visual" box.
  • Voiceover: If in Money Mode, press the microphone button to record the line.
  • Music: Upload a background track in the "Audio Mixer" section at the top.

Step 4: Render & Download

Click "RENDER FINAL VIDEO". Wait for the process to finish.
Important: Do not switch tabs while rendering.
